For today's dish, we will be making a famous and well known dish from Pakistan and it is called :

Chicken Makhni Handi

So what is Chicken Makhni Handi?

Chicken Makhni Handi is a rich and creamy Pakistani curry made with tender chicken, butter, cream, and aromatic spices, slow-cooked in a traditional handi (clay pot) for a deep,
authentic flavor. It combines the velvety texture of butter chicken with the robust taste of Pakistani handi-style cooking. Served with naan or rice, it’s a beloved dish known for its lusci
ous, mildly spiced gravy.

Ingredients:

For the Chicken and Base:

  • ½ kg chicken (boneless)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • ½ cup fresh cream
  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

Spices and Seasonings:

  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ cumin powder
  • ½ garam masala
  • Salt for taste
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

For Garnishing:

  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander
  • *Best served with Garlic Naan or Roti*

Method:

  1. Heat Butter & Oil:
    • In a handi (or deep pan), heat 2 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp oil on medium flame.
  2. Sauté onions & ginger-garlic:
    • Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
    • Add 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Cook tomato puree:
    • Pour in 3 blended tomatoes and cook until the oil separates (8–10 minutes).
  4. Add spices:
    • Mix in 1 tsp red chili powder, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, and salt.
    • Stir well and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add chicken & cook:
    • Add ½ kg chicken and cook until it changes color and is lightly browned (5–7 minutes).
  6. Incorporate yogurt:
    • Lower the heat, add 1 cup whisked yogurt, and stir continuously to prevent curdling.
    • Cook until the oil separates (5 minutes).
  7. Add cream & finish cooking:
    • Pour in ½ cup fresh cream and mix well.
    • Cover and simmer on low heat for 10 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  8. Add kasuri methi & garam masala:
    • Crush 1 tbsp kasuri methi between your hands and add it to the handi.
    • prinkle ½ tsp garam masala, stir, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  9. Garnish & serve:
    • Garnish with 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander.
    • Serve hot with naan or roti!
